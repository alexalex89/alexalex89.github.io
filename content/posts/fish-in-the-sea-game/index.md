A small browser game created by my son with the help of AI: Navigate your fish through the ocean, collect all the coins, and watch out for the oil spills!

## Controls

- **Arrow up / Arrow down** (or **W / S**): Swim up and down
- **Touch**: Steer toward your finger position (mobile)
