A small browser game developed by my son using Claude Code: Jump from rooftop to rooftop, swing on ropes, and deliver the briefcase safely - but watch out, don't fall off the skyscrapers!

## Controls

- **Arrow keys left/right**: Move
- **Arrow up / Space**: Jump
- **Double Space**: Double jump
- **Slide on wall + Space**: Wall jump
- **Rope**: Automatically grabbed
- **Space on rope**: Jump off
